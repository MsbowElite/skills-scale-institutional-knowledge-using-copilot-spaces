# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile processes, remove impediments, and coach teams on agile practices. They ensure the team follows agreed processes and continuously improves.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint ceremonies (standups, planning, retrospectives, reviews)
- Remove blockers and impediments to team progress
- Coach team on agile practices and self-organization
- Shield team from external disruptions
- Track and visualize team velocity and process metrics
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and flow
- Ensure adherence to agile principles and practices
- Create a sustainable pace of delivery
- Improve team collaboration and communication

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- One-on-ones with team members to identify blockers
- Coordination with other Scrum Masters and Project Managers
- Retrospective action items and process improvements

---

## Quality Analyst (QA/Testing)

### Role Summary
Quality Analysts ensure software meets quality standards through systematic testing, defect tracking, and quality advocacy. They bridge the gap between requirements and delivered functionality.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans and test cases
- Perform manual and automated testing (functional, regression, integration)
- Identify, document, and track defects
- Validate acceptance criteria are met
- Collaborate with developers on testability and bug fixes
- Advocate for quality throughout the development lifecycle
- Participate in release sign-off decisions

### Goals
- Ensure high-quality releases with minimal defects
- Reduce post-release issues and customer-reported bugs
- Improve test coverage and automation
- Shorten feedback loops on quality issues

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews with developers and Product Managers
- Bug reports and defect triage meetings
- Release readiness assessments
- Quality metrics reporting (defect rates, test coverage)

---

## UX Designer/Researcher

### Role Summary
UX Designers and Researchers ensure products are user-centered, intuitive, and deliver excellent user experiences. They conduct research, create designs, and validate solutions with users.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research (interviews, surveys, usability testing)
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Design user flows and information architecture
- Establish and maintain design systems and UI patterns
- Collaborate with Product Managers on feature requirements
- Validate designs through user testing and feedback
- Work with developers to ensure design implementation fidelity

### Goals
- Maximize user satisfaction and product usability
- Reduce friction in user workflows
- Ensure consistent, accessible user experiences
- Base design decisions on user research and data

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critiques
- User research findings and personas
- Prototypes and design specifications for developers
- Usability testing reports and recommendations

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure, tooling, and automation that enables reliable, efficient software delivery. They bridge development and operations.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code (IaC) and cloud resources
- Monitor system health, performance, and reliability
- Automate deployment, scaling, and recovery processes
- Implement security best practices in infrastructure
- Troubleshoot production issues and incidents
- Optimize build and deployment times

### Goals
- Achieve fast, reliable, automated deployments
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Reduce manual operational overhead
- Enable developers to deploy confidently and frequently

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure and pipeline design reviews
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Deployment coordination and release planning
- Platform updates and tooling improvements

---

## Support/Customer Success

### Role Summary
Support and Customer Success teams are the voice of the customer, helping users succeed with the product while gathering feedback to inform product improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Respond to customer inquiries and troubleshoot issues
- Escalate bugs and feature requests to engineering
- Document common issues and maintain knowledge base
- Gather customer feedback and usage insights
- Participate in beta testing and early access programs
- Provide input on release notes and customer communications
- Track customer satisfaction and support metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Reduce time to resolution for customer issues
- Improve product quality through customer feedback
- Enable customer self-service through documentation

### Typical Communication
- Bug reports and feature requests to Product/Engineering
- Customer-facing release notes and announcements
- Escalations for critical customer issues
- Feedback sessions on upcoming features and changes

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads ensure products and systems are secure by design, identifying vulnerabilities, establishing security practices, and responding to security incidents.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling
- Perform security testing and vulnerability assessments
- Define and enforce security policies and standards
- Review code for security vulnerabilities
- Respond to security incidents and coordinate remediation
- Ensure compliance with security frameworks and regulations
- Educate team on secure coding practices

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities in production
- Maintain compliance with security standards
- Minimize security incident impact and recovery time
- Foster a security-aware culture

### Typical Communication
- Security review findings and recommendations
- Threat models and risk assessments
- Incident reports and post-incident reviews
- Security training and awareness updates

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts turn data into insights that inform product decisions, measure success, and identify opportunities. They work closely with Product Managers and stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Design and implement analytics tracking and instrumentation
- Create dashboards and reports for key metrics
- Analyze user behavior and product usage patterns
- Conduct A/B testing and experiment analysis
- Identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities in data
- Collaborate on defining success metrics and KPIs
- Support data-driven decision making across teams

### Goals
- Enable data-driven product and business decisions
- Provide timely, accurate insights on product performance
- Identify opportunities for optimization and growth
- Ensure data quality and reliability

### Typical Communication
- Metric definitions and KPI reviews
- Analysis reports and dashboards
- Experiment results and recommendations
- Data requests and custom analysis for stakeholders
